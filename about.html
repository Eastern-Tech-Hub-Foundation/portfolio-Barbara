Barbara Asiamah

Data Analyst

Transforming data into actionable insights with expertise in digital skills training, programming, and web development.

Barbara Asiamah is a dedicated and experienced Data Analyst with a strong background in digital skills training, programming, and web development. She holds a BSc in Information Technology from the University of Ghana and has consistently demonstrated her ability to transform data into meaningful insights. Throughout her career, Barbara has developed and facilitated numerous training programs, enhanced participants' digital skills, and contributed to various educational initiatives aimed at improving computer literacy among young learners.

Her professional experience includes roles as a Data Analyst at Insurerity Digital, where she specialized in data cleaning, exploration, and visualization using Python and Google technologies. She also served as a Graduate Trainee in Data Science at the German Institute of Business and Technology, gaining expertise in data science and cloud computing with tools such as Google Data Studio, Microsoft Azure, and SQL. Additionally, Barbara has hands-on experience in web development, having worked on projects involving React and Angular, and participated in the creation of a payment application and an inventory management system.

Beyond her technical roles, Barbara is an active community volunteer and leader. She has co-organized events for PyData Ghana, mentored participants at Rails Girls Ghana workshops, and led the Google Developer Students Club at her university. Her volunteer efforts extend to teaching computer literacy and programming to underprivileged youth, showcasing her commitment to leveraging technology for social impact. With a passion for data, continuous learning, and community engagement, Barbara Asiamah is poised to make significant contributions to any forward-thinking organization.
